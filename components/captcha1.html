Are CAPTCHAs Getting Too Smart — Or Just Too Cruel?

Every year, security teams promise the new CAPTCHA will be "seamless" and "frictionless." Every year, you spend 40 seconds identifying fire hydrants in low-resolution thumbnails. The technology changes. The frustration does not.

The truth is, distinguishing humans from bots has become an arms race. Modern bots can now solve image puzzles with 97% accuracy. Engineers are forced to find increasingly human things for humans to do — things that require intuition, hesitation, and apparently, graduate-level combinatorics.
